DRINKS

Americano

Is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio, resulting in a drink that retains the complex flavors of espresso, but in a lighter way

Espresso

Is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A latte or caffè latte is a milk coffee that is made up of one or two shots of espresso, lots of steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top.
